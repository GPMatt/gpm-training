Employee Submits CC Receipt

Staff member completes the Google Form after making a company card purchase — attaches a photo of the receipt, logs the property/owner, amount, and description.
🔗 Google Form (accessible on any device)

📁
Drive & Sheet Logging
Live
Form submission triggers a Google Apps Script that files the receipt photo into an organized Drive folder and appends a row to the Billing Sheet — ready for the monthly cycle.
📂 Drive folder organized by owner/month
📊 Billing Sheet row: owner, amount, description, date, status
Monthly Billing Cycle
📨
Trigger: "Statement Ready" Email
Building
An incoming email with the subject "your statement is ready to view" fires the billing cycle. Google Apps Script watches for this email and kicks off the pipeline automatically.
📬 GAS email trigger on automation@ account
⚡ No manual intervention needed to start
👥
Group Receipts by Owner
Building
GAS reads all pending rows from the Billing Sheet and groups them by property owner — each owner becomes one invoice containing all their receipts for the cycle.
🗂️ Pending rows only (not previously billed)
🏠 One invoice per owner, multiple line items possible
🤖
Gemini Description Cleanup
Building
Raw receipt descriptions (often shorthand or messy) are passed to Gemini AI, which rewrites them into clean, professional language suitable for owner-facing invoices.
✏️ "HD plumb tape misc" → "Plumbing materials — supply tape"
🔑 Gemini API key required in Config
👁️
Human Review Gate
Building
Before any invoice is created, Alaina reviews every row in the Sheet — cleaned descriptions, amounts, and owner assignments. Each row is marked Approved or Rejected. The pipeline waits.
✅ Approved rows proceed to QB invoice creation
❌ Rejected rows are skipped and flagged for follow-up
100% review required during initial rollout — no invoice fires without Alaina's explicit approval.
🧾
QuickBooks Invoice Creation
Building
GAS calls the QuickBooks API to create an invoice for each approved owner group. QB is now authorized. Customer ID mapping is the next technical step.
🔐 QB OAuth: authorized ✓
🗺️ Next: pull QB Customer IDs, map to owners in Sheet
📝 One QB invoice per owner per cycle
Failures are non-fatal — if one invoice fails, the batch continues. All failures are logged for review.
📤
Invoice Delivery
Building
Created invoices are emailed to Alaina for records and to the AppFolio intake address, which books them into the property management system automatically.
📧 alaina@greenpropertymgt.com
🏢 greenproperty11@invoices.appfolio.com
